Privacy Policy

Daily Wealth Building (@DailyWealthBuilding) · Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

@DailyWealthBuilding ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our YouTube channel, TikTok account, and associated websites.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not operate a website that collects personal data. We do not have user accounts, registration forms, or data collection mechanisms. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information directly.

3. Information Collected by Third-Party Platforms

When you interact with our content on YouTube or TikTok, those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • YouTube: Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok: TikTok Privacy Policy

We access aggregated analytics data (views, watch time, demographics) provided by these platforms to improve our content. This data does not identify individual users.

4. Cookies

Our associated GitHub Pages website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. Any cookies present are set by GitHub Pages infrastructure, not by us.

5. Affiliate Links

We participate in affiliate programs. When you click affiliate links, the destination websites may set their own cookies and collect data per their privacy policies. We receive only aggregated commission data — not your personal information.

6. Analytics

We use YouTube Analytics and TikTok Analytics (aggregated, anonymized platform-provided data) to understand content performance. No personally identifiable information is accessed through these tools.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for audiences aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. GDPR Rights (EU/UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, or delete personal data. As we do not collect personal data directly, these rights are primarily exercised through YouTube and TikTok's own privacy tools.

9. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers. All content is hosted on YouTube (Google) and TikTok platforms, which maintain industry-standard security measures.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

11. Contact
